Birds of a Feather

Birds of a Feather is solitare card game created by Todd Neller. The rules are simple, and although some deals may be challenging, just remember that over 99% of them are solvable!


Download Birds of a Feather. (These types of files can harm your computer, but this one won't!) You need to have java downloaded for the game to work.


Controls: Click on a card and drag it onto the desired stack. Press the 'u' key to undo a move.


This game was made in collaboration with Han Gong and Nick Wallace, with aid and specifications provided by Todd Neller.
